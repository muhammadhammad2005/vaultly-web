Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Vaultly collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use the app.

Overview

This Privacy Policy explains what data Vaultly may collect, how it may be used, when it may be shared, and how users can request deletion. Privacy questions can be sent to vaultly.app@gmail.com.

Data We Collect

As a secure expense tracker, we prioritize minimizing data collection. We only collect the minimal required data to provide core functionality, such as syncing your authorized budgets and transactions (if cloud backup is enabled).

How We Use Data

Any data collected is strictly used to maintain the app functionality, sync your structured data across devices, and improve your financial tracking experience.

How Data May Be Shared

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. Your financial data is treated with utmost security and only shared in compliance with lawful requests by public authorities.

Retention & Deletion

You have full control over your data. Upon requesting account deletion natively in the app or via email, all cloud-synced files and associated metadata are immediately flagged for permanent removal from our secure servers.

Security and Your Choices

All private data is safeguarded by strict authentication layers. You control App Lock via PIN and Biometrics, ensuring that locally stored numbers remain protected.

Children's Privacy & Policy Updates

This app is not targeted towards children under 13. We reserve the right to modify this privacy policy at any time, so please review it frequently.
