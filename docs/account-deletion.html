Account Deletion

This page explains how to request deletion of your Vaultly account, what information is removed, and what may be retained when required.

Request deletion Delete inside the app Retention details

Request Deletion by Email

Send an email to vaultly.app@gmail.com.
Use the subject line Vaultly Account Deletion Request.
Include the email address used by your Vaultly account so the request can be verified.
Start Deletion Request

Delete Inside The App

If you still have access to the app, open Settings > Account > Delete Account to permanently remove your synced account and cloud data directly from the app.

What Gets Deleted

Account profile data.
Synchronized transactions, budgets, recurring plans, and preferences associated with the account.
Push device registrations associated with the account.
Google Play entitlement records linked to the deleted account, except where records must be temporarily retained for billing or fraud-prevention purposes.

Important Notes

Deleting your Vaultly account does not automatically cancel an active Google Play subscription. Subscription management and cancellation remain in Google Play.
Vaultly may temporarily retain limited records when required for fraud prevention, billing disputes, security investigations, or legal compliance. These cases are handled for the minimum period reasonably required.
