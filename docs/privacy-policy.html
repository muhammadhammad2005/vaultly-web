Vaultly Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Vaultly collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use the app.

Account information Financial data Notifications & purchases

Overview

This Privacy Policy explains what data Vaultly may collect, how it may be used, when it may be shared, and how users can request deletion. Privacy questions can be sent to vaultly.app@gmail.com.

Data We Collect

Account information: email address, authentication state, and profile details you provide.
Financial content: transactions, budgets, recurring plans, categories, summaries, and app preferences saved to your account.
Security settings: app-lock preferences, biometric preference, and related device-level security choices.
Notification data: push notification token, notification preference, and time zone when notifications are enabled.
Purchase data: product identifiers, purchase tokens, and entitlement status used to verify Google Play subscriptions, lifetime purchases, and premium access.
Support data: messages or details you share when contacting support.

How We Use Data

To create and maintain your account.
To sync your data across supported devices.
To deliver reminders, summaries, and push notifications.
To verify Google Play purchases and manage premium entitlements.
To protect account access, assist with account recovery, and improve security.
To provide customer support, diagnose issues, and prevent abuse, fraud, or unauthorized activity.

How Data May Be Shared

Supabase is used for account authentication, data storage, synchronization, and server functions.
Google Play is used for billing and purchase verification.
Firebase Cloud Messaging is used to deliver push notifications.
Data may also be disclosed if required to comply with law, legal process, or to protect users, the service, or the public.

Retention & Deletion

Vaultly keeps account and app data for as long as needed to provide the service, maintain your account, support legitimate business operations, resolve disputes, and meet legal or security obligations.
You can delete your account from inside the app or by using the account deletion page.
When an account deletion request is completed, Vaultly removes synchronized account data unless temporary retention is required for fraud prevention, billing disputes, security investigations, chargeback handling, or legal compliance.
Deleting a Vaultly account does not automatically cancel an active Google Play subscription. Subscription management and cancellation remain in Google Play.

Security and Your Choices

Vaultly uses authenticated services for synced account data, disables Android backups, blocks cleartext traffic, and supports app lock, PIN, biometric unlock, and secure-screen protections on supported devices.
You can review and manage app preferences inside the app.
You can disable notifications from inside the app or through your device settings.
You can request account deletion from inside the app or through the public account deletion page.

Children's Privacy & Policy Updates

Vaultly is not directed to children and is not intended for children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal data in violation of applicable law, contact vaultly.app@gmail.com.
Vaultly may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected on this page by updating the effective date above.
