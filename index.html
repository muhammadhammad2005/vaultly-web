Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about Vaultly.

Is Vaultly available on iPhone? +

Vaultly is currently available for Android devices. You can find us on the Google Play Store. An iOS version may be considered for the future.

What does Vaultly Pro unlock? +

Vaultly Pro removes the 1-budget limit giving you unlimited tracking. It also unlocks powerful tools such as local home widgets, App Lock (PIN and Biometrics), PDF/JSON/CSV data exports, recurring bill tracking with reminders, and the ability to customize your app colors.

Does Vaultly support recurring bills and reminders? +

Yes. Vaultly Pro lets you set up automatic, repeating plans for a salary, subscriptions, or utility bills. We tie these together with push notifications and local reminders so you'll never miss an important date.

Can I export or restore my data? +

Absolutely. You own your data. Pro users can export all transaction history in CSV format for spreadsheets, or JSON for full account backups. You can restore data back into Vaultly at any time from CSV files.

How does account deletion work? +

You can instantly initiate account deletion via the in-app settings (Settings > Account > Delete Account), which removes your synced data. You can also review our Account Deletion and Privacy Policy pages for full compliance.
